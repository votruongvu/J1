# CE-001 — Bill of Quantities (BOQ): Quantity Schedule

Project: River Crossing Bridge Substructure
Document Type: Bill of Quantities (BOQ / NCR / Non-Conformance Report aliases applied where relevant)
Revision: A
Issued: 2026-03-10
Prepared by: Quantities Engineer, M. Hannah

This bill of quantities lists priced items for the substructure
package. Quantities are net of voids and openings. Unit rates are
exclusive of value added tax and include labour, plant, materials,
and contractor's overhead unless explicitly noted.
