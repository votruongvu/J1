## Specification extract — Section 03 30 00 Cast-in-place Concrete

* Concrete grade shall be C30/37 minimum for all reinforced
  concrete elements at substructure level, including slab S-205,
  abutment walls, and column bases.
* Concrete grade C40/50 is permitted as a re-pour grade where a
  non-conformance corrective action has been formally accepted by
  the project quality manager.
* Cover to reinforcement shall be 40 mm for elements in exposure
  class XC4 (cyclic wet-dry conditions). The slab S-205 is
  classified XC4 and the 40 mm cover requirement applies.
* Cube testing for compressive strength shall be performed at 7
  and 28 days. The acceptance criterion at 28 days is the
  characteristic strength of the specified grade with a maximum
  individual deficiency of 4 N/mm².

## Specification extract — Section 03 20 00 Reinforcement

* Reinforcement steel shall be grade B500B unless explicitly
  noted otherwise on drawings.
* Mechanical couplers shall be of a type approved by the design
  engineer; see BOQ row 20.03.
* Inspection requirement: a reinforcement inspection shall be
  carried out prior to every pour. The inspection shall be
  recorded on the project quality form QM-12.
* Testing requirement: tensile testing of mechanical couplers
  shall be carried out at the rate of one in every 50 installed
  couplers. Test results shall be filed in the project quality
  records.

## Specification extract — Section 02 50 00 Earthworks

* Excavation tolerance shall be -50 mm / +25 mm relative to the
  founding level shown on D-200.
* Backfill shall be compacted to 95% of the maximum dry density
  (MDD) determined by the modified Proctor test.

## Inspection and Testing Requirements (cross-section summary)

* Concrete pour inspections: per pour, before placement, see
  specification 03 30 00 and CE-002.
* Reinforcement inspections: per pour, before placement, see
  specification 03 20 00.
* Cube strength testing: 7 and 28 days, per pour, recorded
  against drawing/section.
* Coupler tensile testing: one in every 50, per specification
  03 20 00.
* As-built drawing updates: required after any corrective
  action; see NCR-007 in CE-003.
